Cell phone addiction among teenagers is a growing concern for families across the country. With the average teen spending over 7 hours a day on screens, it's more important than ever to establish healthy boundaries.

## Understanding the Problem

The first step in addressing teen cell phone addiction is understanding why it happens. Social media platforms are designed to be addictive, using variable reward schedules that keep users coming back for more.

## Practical Strategies

1. **Set clear boundaries** — Establish phone-free zones and times in your home
2. **Model good behavior** — Children learn from watching their parents
3. **Use built-in tools** — Both iOS and Android offer screen time management features
4. **Encourage alternative activities** — Sports, arts, and family outings provide healthy alternatives
