Every single family will face its own set of challenges along this journey in life. No matter who you are, how much money you make, where you live, who you marry and even if you are the “All American” family… you are bound to encounter a few bumps along the way. Many families today face things such as depression, anxiety, addiction, financial hardship, and really a number of differing issues right within their own home.

In this powerful round table discussion, we will introduce you to Jeff and Diane Shephard. They open up to reveal the ugly truth of how they nearly lost their marriage through the struggles of addiction, mental illness and a very tragic accident where they almost lost their son.

In this conversation the Shephard’s not only discuss what they have gone through, but also what steps they have taken to rescue their marriage AND what they continue to do to support their entire family and protect their marriage!
