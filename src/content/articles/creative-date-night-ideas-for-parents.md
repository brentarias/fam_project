As parents, it's easy to let date nights fall by the wayside. Between work, school activities, and bedtime routines, finding time for each other can feel impossible. But maintaining your connection as a couple is one of the best things you can do for your family.

## At-Home Date Nights

You don't need a babysitter to reconnect. Try these at-home ideas:

- **Cook a new recipe together** — Pick a cuisine neither of you has tried
- **Backyard stargazing** — Grab some blankets and enjoy the night sky
- **Game night for two** — Break out the board games after the kids are in bed

## Adventure Dates

When you can get out of the house:

- **Explore a local farmers market** — Sample local foods and discover something new
- **Take a class together** — Pottery, cooking, or dancing
- **Volunteer together** — Give back to your community while spending quality time
